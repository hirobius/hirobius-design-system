Add four presentational primitives that consumers hit immediately: **Spinner** (indeterminate loading indicator), **Skeleton** (content-loading shimmer, `text`/`rectangular`/`circular`), **Progress** (linear determinate/indeterminate bar), and **Avatar** (image with initials fallback). All token-driven, accessible (`role="status"`/`progressbar"`/`img`), and honor `prefers-reduced-motion`.
